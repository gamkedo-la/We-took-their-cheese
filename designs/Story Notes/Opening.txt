Player meets mouse wizzard who hires player to run trade

Game opens up with a fancy guy dropping his wallet. 
You have choice to keep wallet or return it. 
If you return it, he tells you to meet him at his office. He'd like to offer you a job. 
If you keep it, the game just starts and you skip the tutorial. 

You arrive at the world map and go "Ahgahhh!! what the hell is this?"
Mouse wizzard reveals himself. "Since you took my wallet, I decided to return the favor."
